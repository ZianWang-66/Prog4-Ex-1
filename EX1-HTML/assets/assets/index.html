The characters
Shinnosuke Nohara (Shinchan) Misae Nohara (Mom) Hiroshi Nohara (Dad)
The mischievous 5-year-old protagonist known for his hilarious antics and love of choco chips. Shinchan's short-tempered but loving mother, always trying to keep him in line. The laid-back father who works at a company and enjoys his evening beer.
Himawari (Sister) Yukino Nohara (Shiro) Toru Kazama
The youngest of the Noharas. Loves shiny objects and brand name goods. Smiles sideways, like Shinchan The family dog — small, fluffy, and sometimes the most sensible member of the household. The smart and responsible friend of Shinchan, often the voice of reason in the group.
